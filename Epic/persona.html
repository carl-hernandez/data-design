User Persona

  • Name: Tammy Day
  • Age: 20
  • Gender: Female
  • Profession: Retail worker
  • Technology: Cell phone (Google Pixel), Dell Laptop with Windows 10

    • Wants to buy Magic: the Gathering cards at a fair price
